Who We Are

ADR Power Systems, Inc. was founded by David Arata and Richard DeBord in October of 2015. Operations began in a small warehouse near the Channel District in Tampa, Florida, and focused on marine transmission services for recreational and commercial fishing vessels ranging from small boats to mega yachts. Expert workmanship, coupled with rapid turn-around time, catapulted the company to broaden its presence to include the State of Florida, Eastern Seaboard, and the Caribbean.

Today, ADR Power Systems, Inc. possesses over one hundred years of combined Twin Disc experience and has expanded its service offering to include maritime electronic controls, marine engines, and industrial transmission repairs, among others. The company is composed of a balanced blend of skilled resources that act as a single-synchronized unit to minimize downtime and keep customers focused on their mission. ADR’s commitment to excellence has allowed them to become authorized dealers for ZF, Glendinning, & NACD.

What We Do

Transmission Inspections, Repairs, & Rebuilds

ADR Power Systems is the leading expert in Marine Transmission rebuilds whether we are working meticulously inside the boat or rebuilding at our headquarters. We have full rebuild capabilities for all Twin Disc, ZF, Velvet Drive, Borg Warner, and Arneson transmissions. Our rebuilds come with a full warranty as well as our industry leading 24/7 support. ADR offers removals, overhauls, and installations for any of our customers.

Outboard Engines

We are authorized Platinum Dealers of the recently developed 300HP High Performance Diesel Outboards – CXO300. These units produce 480 ft.lb of torque and consume 25% less fuel than it’s gas-powered equivalents.

Marine Diesel Engine Inspections, Repairs, & Rebuilds

Our team of technicians, with over 100 years of Marine Caterpillar Engine experience, execute projects ranging from C2.2 Diesel Marine Generators to 3600 series engines and everything in between. We also offer Maintenance Agreements to keep your investment running nominally.

Electronic Diagnostics & Repairs

Electronic control issues can be incredibly complex and difficult to diagnose and fix. ADR’s factory trained technicians are capable to work on any control system issues you may come across. ADR specializes in Twin Disc EC300’s, 250’s, 200’s and 100’s. We are also proud to be an authorized dealer for Glendinning and ZF Controls.

PTO/HPTO Inspections, Repairs, & Rebuilds

ADR Power Systems also services many PTO/HPTO units. Twin Disc rebuilds. WPT rebuilds NACD rebuilds. Rockford rebuilds. Logan rebuilds. Twin Disc torque converter rebuilds (CO, CF, IF).
